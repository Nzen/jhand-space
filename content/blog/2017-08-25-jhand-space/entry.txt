I really needed a new place for experimentation, fun, and writing. My old site, [joeahand.com](https://joeahand.com/), was nice and a lot of fun to build. But it was not good for blogging or adding random frontend experiements.

* JS Framework: [Choo](https://github.com/choojs/choo)
* CSS: [tachyons](http://tachyons.io/)
* Analytics: [fair analytics](https://github.com/vesparny/fair-analytics)
